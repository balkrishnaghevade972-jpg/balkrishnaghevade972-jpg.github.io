Diet Specifications for Moms (Before & After Pregnancy)

Pre-Pregnancy Weight Based Diet Guide

Stage Recommendations
Before Pregnancy (Pre-Conception) • Folate-rich foods (spinach, legumes)
• Whole grains & millets
• Nuts, seeds, healthy fats
• Protein foods (eggs, chicken, dal)
• Balanced calcium & iron
More Info
During Pregnancy • Add ~350 calories (2nd & 3rd trimester)
• Vegetables (300g), fruits (150g)
• Pulses (80g), nuts (40g), dairy (400 ml)
• Lean meat/fish or plant protein
More Info
After Pregnancy (Post-Delivery) • High-protein meals
• Iron-rich foods
• Hydration (water, soups)
• Healthy fats (nuts, ghee in moderation)
• Small frequent meals for breastfeeding
